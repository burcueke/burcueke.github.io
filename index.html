# Burcu Eke Rubini I build statistical modeling frameworks for structured, dependent systems like networks, longitudinal processes, survey and census data, and predictive systems where standard independence assumptions break down. --- ## Focus - **Modeling under dependence:** Relational, longitudinal, and hierarchical systems. - **Network formation & relational inference:** Structural modeling of interconnected entities. - **Missing data & imputation systems:** MAR/MNAR mechanisms, large-scale survey and census applications. - **Predictive modeling:** Calibration, stability, and uncertainty quantification. - **Statistical computing:** R, Python, and SAS. --- ## Selected Work - **Multi-criteria Evaluation of Health News Stories** *Decision Support Systems (2024)* A structured, decision-analytic modeling framework for evaluating health news reliability under uncertainty. - **Crisis Probability & Policy Intervention Modeling** Panel-based modeling of foreign exchange, inflation, and output crises incorporating structural breaks and heterogeneous treatment effects. - **Network & Relational Systems Modeling** Statistical models for dynamic and dependent multi-agent systems. --- ## Leadership - **President-Elect**, Caucus for Women in Statistics and Data Science (CWS) - **Secretary (Founding Leadership Team)**, International Turkish Statistical Association (ITSA) --- ## Contact
